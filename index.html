Services

Store Setup & Theme Customization

Building an elegant, well functioning Shopify store is important. I can customize your store with theme edits or add custom functionality to suit the uniqueness of your business. I will guide you through theme choice and point out advantages, be it a free theme or a premier theme such as Out Of The Sandbox's Turbo. Setup includes training so that you feel confident with your online store.

App Integration or Replication

Integrating apps to stores tremendously scales functionality and features. There's a host of options such as integrating Xero and TaxJar for accounting and sales tax, or providing powerful marketing campaigns through email and social media. Additionally, I replicate certain app functionality in order to save you monthly fees as well as speed up your store, such as adding swatches to filters or adding a gift wrap option.

Site Design & Branding

A beautifully designed website with engaging content and memorable branding will turn casual shoppers into forever customers. I create online stores that convey a brand's message, profiles products beautifully, and looks trustworthy.

Theme Updates & Maintenance

Once your store is live it’s important to maintain it well. I perform theme updates when themes have new version releases, often requiring manual code intervention. I can assist you in updating your store with new products and content, or taking advantage of newly available features.
