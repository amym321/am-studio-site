Services

Store Setup & Theme Customization

Building an elegant, well functioning Shopify store is important. We can customize your store with theme edits or added custom functionality to suit the uniqueness of your business. Setup includes training and support so that you feel confident with your online store.

Site Design & Branding

A beautifully designed website with engaging content and memorable branding will turn casual shoppers into forever customers. We create online stores that conveys your brand's message, profiles products beautifully, and looks trustworthy.

App Installations

Every business is unique. Integrating apps with ecommerce stores tremendously scales functions and features. There's a host of options such as connecting to Xero or Quickbooks for accounting, or providing powerful email marketing and social media campaigns.

Theme Updates & Maintenance

Once your store is live it’s important to maintain it well. We perform theme updates when themes have new version releases, often requiring manual code intervention. We can assist you in updating your store with new products and content, or taking advantage of newly available features.
